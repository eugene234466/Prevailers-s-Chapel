About Us

Prevailers Chapel International is a vibrant community of believers committed to experiencing God's transforming power and sharing his love with the world. We are a family where everyone belongs,growing together in faith, worship and service. We also highly acknowledge soul-winning.

Our mission is to empower people to discover their purpose, devlop their faith, and make a difference in their communities. Through dynamic worship, biblical teaching and genuine fellowship, we create an atmosphere where lives are changed and destinies are fulfilled.
